Detailed Description

Architecture of the seven_seg_control.

The seven_seg_control controls the output of the seven segment displays. To do this the modules uses clk_div_seven_seg to divide the clock to a suitable speed to the seven_seg_sel to switch. This module also uses bin2bcd to convert the numbers from binary to BCD. The BCD is then converted to seven segment numbers and outputted on the seven_seg_out.
